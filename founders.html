Our Founders

Our Founders


LATE Dr. C.P.PRABHAKAR "MAWTHIRI"


An oak stood amidst the forest
With its widespread boughs of wisdom
Sheltered a cluster of nests
Where Life nestled in its shade
Fruits and seeds of its virtue
Clinged to its branches & were nurtured
By the soil in which penetrated its roots.
From the skies that it touched
To the ground where it would stand
Blossoms had gathered all around
As it was blessed by God's own hand
The world has all mortals
Few like him leave behind imprints.



LATE Mrs. KIRON PRABHAKAR


God took the strength of a mountain,
The majesty of a tree,
The warmth of a summer sun,
The calm of a quiet sea,
The generous soul of nature,
The wisdom of ages,
The joy of a morning in spring,
The patience of eternity,
The depth of a family need,
The will to surge ahead,
The gift of words well-said,
Then, God combined all these qualities,
And, When there was nothing more to add,
He knew his masterpiece was complete,

From their portraits, they seem to be telling us
'Grieve not, we are here with you,
We are in every step you take forward,
We are in every dream come true,
We are in the developing education,
We are in every success you pursue,'
Let us emulate and follow the footprints of these great souls.
Let us immortalize them by our efforts to realize their ideals.
