ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Registration For Admission

Names are registered for admission to the school in the month of January every year. Registration for a particular class does not mean that the child would be admitted to that class.

A child who has attained the age of 3 years as on March 31 of the relevant year is admitted to class Pre-School, 4 years for Pre-Primary, 5 years for class-I as per the rules, regulations and criteria displayed at the school notice board at the start of the admission procedure of that particular academic session. The application for admission must be accompanied by a birth certificate issued by the appropriate Municipal authority.


ADMISSION CRITERIA FOR ENTRY LEVEL CLASSES

(PRE-SCHOOL, PRE-PRIMARY AND CLASS ONE)

2017-2018 (FOR E. W. S.)


ADMISSION FOR EWS AND DISADVANTAGED GROUP CATEGORY ENTRY LEVEL CLASSES WILL BE CONDUCTED ON THE BASIS OF HON'BLE DELHI HIGH COURT JUDGEMENT DATED 31/01/2012 IN wp (c) no. 636/2012 and wp (c) no. 40/12 and VIDE CIRCULAR NO. F.15 (110)/DE/ACT/2011/7563-74 DATED 13/02/2012 ISSUED BY THE DIRECTORATE OF EDUCATION (ACT BRANCH) NCR DELHI.


FOLLOWING IS THE ORDER OF HON'BLE DELHI HIGH COURT.

  • Admission shall first be offered to eligible students belonging to EWS and disadvantaged group residing within 1 KM. of the specific schools.
  • In case the vacancies remain unfilled, students residing within 3 Kms. of the school shall be admitted.
  • If there are still vacancies, then the admission shall be offered to other students residing within 6 Kms. of the institutions.
  • Students residing beyond 6 kms. shall be admitted only in case vacancies remain unfilled even after considering all the students within 6 kms. area.
