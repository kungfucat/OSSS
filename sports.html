Sports

Sports Activities

To boost up the physical growth and development of the students and to infuse in them the spirit of patience and courage, games are given their due share of importance at Oxford. There is a separate allocation in the academic timetable for sports, besides practice sessions before and after school timings. Having adopted Basket Ball as the Prime sport, our school is the seed ground for nourishing talent in the field of Basket Ball. Founder Chairman, Late Dr. C. P. Prabhakar, a keen sportsman himself, was a firm believer that sports are the best activity with which the vigour of the youth can be fruitfully channelised. With this end in view Hansraj Prabhakar Memorial Invitational Basket Ball Tournament was started in the fond memory of Late Sh. Hansraj Prabhakar—the guiding beacon behind this institution. It has been an annual event since 1990 and the prestige gained by this tournament can well be gauged by the participation of teams from various highly reputed schools in the Delhi region. The tournament is held under following four categories —.


Categories
Senior Boys
Senior Girls
Mini Boys (Under 13 years)
Mini Girls (Under 13 years)

At the end of the event, Winners and Runners-up in all the sections are awarded with trophies as well as individual prizes. The Highest scorer, the Most Promising Player and the Best Player are also awarded. In addition there are Free Throw and Three Pointer Competitions too. This year Shubham Prabhakar was adjudged the Most Promising Player and Saurav Yadav the Best Player of the tournament, in the Mini Boys category.
Our school retained the championship trophy in the Mini Boys category for the second consecutive year. Mini Girls bagged the second place.
Oxford has the honour of being among the limited few schools in Delhi, that are the members of the Delhi Basket Ball Association.
Our Annual Sports Day is celebrated in the month of February to commemorate the Birth Anniversary of our venerated visionary Founder Chairman Late Dr. C. P. Prabhakar, 'MawThiri'. It is an Intra-School Athletics Meet for the students of classes Pre-School to V. The events in the Sports Day are :


P.T. drills : Hoop Drill, Ribbon Drill and Scarf Drill
Yoga
Dumbles
Track and Field events
