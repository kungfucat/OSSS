Co-Curricular Activities

At Oxford education is extended beyond the realms of conventional classrooms, thus providing the Oxfordians opportunities for an over-all development of their personalities. In addition to meaningful and rewarding academic education the school offers multifarious co-curricular activities to the students thus successfully tapping their creativity and exploring their talents. The elaborate facilities and infrastructure provides an adequate environment for the mental and physical growth, leading to holistic development of each student. The exposure to co-curricular activities, through Intra-Class and Inter-House Competitions forms a regular part of the curriculum, that helps in shaping the dynamic personalities who emerge winners wherever they go, in whatever they do. In the Pre-Primary and Primary sections mass participation is ensured through Intra-Class Competitions.
